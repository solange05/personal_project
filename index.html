Solange Paniagua

Designer, Full Stack Developer and a little bit of everything

Hi, I'm Sol. Since I was a child, I have had a great passion for technology. Although it is a very broad field, my interests and curiosity led me to study and take programming courses. At the same time, I am a student pursuing a Technical University Degree in Administration at UTN. I consider myself a person with many diverse interests, and I try to dedicate time to each of those things as much as possible. Below, this page is about me and how I am putting some of my knowledge into practice.

A Little About Me

Throughout my short life, as I grew, various hobbies and passions emerged. It started with music, playing an instrument like the violin and then dancing. I also really enjoyed (and still enjoy) studying. My curiosity about how things work led me to study programming, currently focusing on full-stack development. I am also pursuing a technical degree in administration, as this field also interests me.
